About

My name is Vincent Zaballa. I am a biomedical engineer by training and education, and a data scientist by trial and error. My interests lie within the realm of medical devices, ranging from tissue engineering to deep learning diagnostics, and the practical implementations of those technologies.

When I'm not writing about medical devices or prototyping deep learning models, I take freelance work to help pay the bills. Please get in touch if you would like to discuss any data science or deep learning projects you have and would like assistance with.
